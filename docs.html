Zealer Dashboard

v2.0.0 — Engineering Manager + Engineer cockpit for Jira + Sentry

Roles

Choose your role on first launch (or in Settings → Your role).

  • Engineering Manager — full squad view with team filters. Burndown, team timesheet, and all sprint stories by default. Manage your squad member list from Settings.
  • Engineer — personal view by default. A Me / Squad toggle appears on sprint tickets, time tracking, estimate charts, and support boards. In Me mode the time-tracking charts switch to a personal time-series view: daily bars per sprint day (or monthly bars for quarterly mode) showing your logged hours, with a paired estimate bar when estimates exist on your tickets.

Engineer progress circles

In Engineer mode, two personal donut charts appear at the top of the Today screen (hidden when you have no assigned work):

  • Sprint — your assigned story points broken down by Done (green), In Progress (blue), and Open (slate). Centre shows done / total pts.
  • Support — your assigned support tickets by status. Centre shows done / total. Always based on your assignments regardless of the Me/Squad toggle.

Screens

  • Today — Alert inbox · Insights (Sentry trend, burndown, time logged, estimate vs actual) · Extra boards · Sentry Issues · Current Sprint
  • Sprint Health — Velocity trend, goal hit rate, carry-over, stale tickets
  • Reliability — Sentry 7-day trend, untriaged spikes, SLA adherence, security tickets

Settings

  • Your role — EM or Engineer. Controls which sections and filters are shown.
  • Jira — base URL, email, API token. Squad project key + extra boards (format: Name|BoardID).
  • Sentry — org slug, base URL, auth token. Add as many tracked views as you like.
  • Squad members (EM only) — curate who appears in the team filter. Once saved, auto-discovery stops updating the list.

Milestones (OKRs & Dev Plans)

Add milestones in Settings as label, label|Display Name, or label|Display Name|Leapsome URL — one per line. Tag backlog tickets with the label in Jira and the milestone card tracks them by ticket count (done/total + %), with a status breakdown, an IN SPRINT badge on tickets in the active sprint, and a click-through listing. Engineer Me/Squad scope applies. Add a Leapsome URL to get an "Open in Leapsome" link; otherwise a small hint reminds you to update Leapsome manually.

Demo / Mock Data Mode

Enable via the toggle in Settings (below your role). Fills every chart with realistic mock data — no API calls are made. Great for demos or exploring the app before adding credentials. Turns off automatically on browser restart.

Sprint Timeline (Gantt)

A Gantt chart in the Insights section showing each sprint ticket as a horizontal bar from its start date to its due date. Sorted by priority then Jira rank. Click any row to open the ticket in Jira. Engineer mode shows only your own tickets.

Splash screen

A short branded splash (navy background, cap icon, ripple animation, then the "Zealer Dashboard" title) plays once per browser session when you first open the panel. It respects your reduced-motion preference.

Privacy Mode

Click 🔒 in the app bar to toggle screen-share privacy. When ON, personal content is masked while dashboards and metrics remain visible.
