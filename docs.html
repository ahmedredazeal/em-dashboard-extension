EM Dashboard — Documentation

Phase 1 (v1.0.0) — Foundation with Jira + Sentry integration, alert rules, and privacy mode.

Alert Rules (Phase 1)

  • velocity_drop — Velocity drops >15% for 2 consecutive sprints
  • sprint_goal_at_risk — Mid-sprint burndown predicts goal miss
  • sentry_spike_untriaged — Sentry issue >24h old, not triaged
  • support_sla_breach — Support ticket aged past SLA

Screens

  • Today — Alert inbox, sprint glance, fresh Sentry spikes
  • Sprint Health — Velocity, goal hit rate, carry-over, stale tickets
  • Reliability — Sentry 7-day trend, untriaged spikes, SLA adherence

Privacy Mode

Click the 🔒 icon in the app bar to toggle screen-share privacy mode. When ON, personal content (1:1 notes, people log, promotion evidence) is masked. Dashboards and metrics remain visible.
