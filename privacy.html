EM Dashboard — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 11, 2026

1. What Data We Access

EM Dashboard accesses:

  • Jira credentials (base URL, email, API token)
  • Sentry credentials (base URL, organization, project, auth token)
  • Sprint data, stories, subtasks, worklogs from your Jira instance
  • Error issues and events from your Sentry instance
  • Locally-stored notes (1:1 logs, people log, growth plans, promotion cycle)

2. Where It's Stored

All data is stored in chrome.storage.local — encrypted at rest by Chrome, stored locally on your device only. No third-party servers. No cloud sync.

3. How It's Used

Credentials are used exclusively to make read-only API calls directly from your browser to your Jira and Sentry instances. No data is transmitted to any third-party service or Zeal servers.

4. No Third-Party Transmission

EM Dashboard makes API calls only to your configured Jira and Sentry instances. No analytics, no tracking, no external data transmission.

5. How to Delete Your Data

Uninstall the extension. All data in chrome.storage.local is deleted automatically when the extension is removed.
