Sentry connection

Connect to Sentry to track error trends and untriaged spikes.

Your Sentry instance URL
Your Sentry organization slug
Paste a Sentry view URL — we'll extract view ID, projects, and environment.
Example: https://zeal.sentry.io/issues/views/205220/?project=6042935...
Click Track on one view to enable the daily issue-count trend chart in the dashboard. Only one view can be tracked at a time.
Create one at Sentry → Settings → Auth Tokens
